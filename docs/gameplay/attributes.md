# Attributes & Player Stats

## Core Attributes

Each character has six core attributes that form the foundation of their power and capabilities.

| Attribute | Description | Primary Functions |
|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Vitality** | Total health points | Determines HP pool |
| **Intelligence** | Mana pool and scaling | Scales Magic Damage (MD) |
| **Endurance** | Energy pool | Fuels physical/non-mage abilities |
| **Strength** | Heavy equipment requirement | Scales AD for heavy weapons; increases HP and block negation |
| **Dexterity** | Nimble equipment requirement | Scales AD for nimble weapons; reduces fall damage and increases speed |
| **Luck** | RNG modifier | Increases crit chance and rare loot drop rates |

## Stat Scaling

Each attribute contributes to character power through weapon scaling multipliers. Attributes are multiplied by weapon-specific scaling values to determine bonus damage output.

For detailed scaling mechanics and how attributes factor into damage calculations, see the [Combat & Damage System](./combat-system.md).
