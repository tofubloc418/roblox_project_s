# Bosses

## Overview

Bosses are unique, high-challenge entities placed on wild islands throughout Project S. Each boss has distinct mechanics, attack patterns, and rewards.

## Characteristics

### Challenge

- **Unique Mechanics:** Each boss features specific mechanics and abilities designed to test player skill
- **Complex Combat:** Bosses require strategy and adaptation to defeat
- **Scaling:** Boss difficulty increases on outer islands

### Rewards

- **Guaranteed High-Tier Loot:** Defeating a boss guarantees valuable equipment and resources
- **Significant EXP:** Major experience gains for defeating bosses
- **Progression Gateway:** Defeating bosses often unlocks access to new islands or vendors

## Encounter Design

- **Arena Design:** Bosses are placed in specific arena locations
- **Health & Resistances:** Bosses have substantial health pools and strategic resistance values
- **Abilities:** Custom ability sets that define each boss's combat style

## Variants

Bosses may appear in variants across different islands with adjusted stats and mechanics to match the local difficulty.
