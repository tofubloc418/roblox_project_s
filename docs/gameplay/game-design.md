# Project S: Full Game Design & Combat System Specification

## I. Overview

Project S is an **Extraction MMORPG** for Roblox that combines the interconnected world-building of *Elden Ring*, the tense extraction loops of *Arc Raiders*, and the survival/combat mechanics of *Valheim* and *League of Legends*. The world is structured as a massive web of interconnected islands where danger and reward scale as players move outward from the center.
