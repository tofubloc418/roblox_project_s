# Core Game Mechanics

## Core Gameplay Loop

* **World Structure:** A web of interconnected islands. Mobs and bosses increase in difficulty the further players venture from the center.
* **Safezones (Village Islands):** Scattered throughout the web. These serve as spawn points and house player stashes for loot and currency.
* **Village Features:** NPC shops, trade hubs, and a colosseum for combat.
* **Exploration:** Players venture into "wild islands" to defeat enemies, conquer bosses, and discover new points of interest.
* **Progression:** Defeating enemies grants EXP. Drops can be sold for currency or used to craft/repair weapons and tools.
