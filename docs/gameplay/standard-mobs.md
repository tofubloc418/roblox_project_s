# Standard Mobs

## Overview

Standard mobs are the basic enemies encountered throughout the wild islands of Project S. They serve as the primary source of experience, currency, and common loot.

## Mechanics

### Scaling

- **Difficulty Scaling:** Mobs scale based on their island location. Mobs further from the center are stronger.
- **Stat Scaling:** Individual mob strength is handled via their internal Armor and Resistance stats.
- **Challenge:** Designed to provide meaningful combat without being impossible to overcome.

### Loot

- **Common Drops:** Currency and basic equipment
- **Crafting Materials:** Resources for weapon and armor crafting/repair
- **Progression:** Defeating mobs in outer islands yields better loot and more EXP

## Behavior

- **Aggression:** Standard mobs respond to player presence and attack on sight
- **Combat AI:** Simple attack patterns and basic abilities
- **Grouping:** Often found in small groups on islands
