# World & Progression

## World Structure

Project S features a web of interconnected islands with scaling difficulty based on distance from the center.

### Island Types

| Type | Purpose | Features |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Village Islands (Safezones)** | Hub areas for rest and trading | NPC shops, trade hubs, colosseum, player stashes |
| **Wild Islands** | Combat and exploration zones | Enemies, bosses, loot, environmental hazards |

### Difficulty Scaling

- Difficulty and loot quality increase the further players venture from the center
- Players can choose their risk/reward by selecting which islands to explore
- Progression is tied to moving outward and conquering higher-difficulty zones

Project S features multiple types of enemies across its islands:

- **[Standard Mobs](./standard-mobs.md)** - Common enemies providing EXP and loot
- **[Bosses](./bosses.md)** - Unique, high-challenge entities with distinct mechanics and rewarding loot

---

## Progression System

### Core Loop

1. **Explore** wild islands outward from the center
2. **Fight** enemies and bosses
3. **Collect** loot drops
4. **Extract** back to a safezone
5. **Upgrade** weapons, armor, and stats with currency and materials

### Experience & Growth

- **EXP Gains:** Defeating enemies grants experience points
- **Stat Growth:** EXP translates to attribute increases
- **Equipment:** Drops can be equipped or sold for currency
- **Crafting:** Gathered materials combine with currency to craft/repair gear

### Risk/Reward

- More dangerous outer islands offer better loot
- Extraction mechanics create tension and meaningful risk
- Multiple progression paths allow for different playstyles
